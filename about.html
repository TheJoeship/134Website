About Us

Coffee Chaos

Coffee Chaos was originally started as an instagram blog by two of my friends at UCSD as a passion project while they were in school and studying together.The first ever Coffee Chaos review was at Lovesong Coffee in North Park, San Diego

Now it's grown into its own full on website that I maintain and work on the reviews with them! We're a group that loves coffee, and we also love to explore San Diego. And most importantly... we hate going to coffee shops that don't have outlets! Imagine going to a brand-new coffee shop, you spend $8 on a Latte and your laptop dies 15 minutes into your study session! We're trying to help people prevent that by giving them comprehensive reviews of some of our San Diego favorites!

How we score:

Coffee Taste
Probably the most obvious category! How does the coffee taste?? We try various drinks (because you can't get just one) and score on how much we actually like these drinks.
Specialties
Here's where we rate how good a place's specialty is! What makes the coffee shop unqiue?? Do they have an orange juice Americano?? Turkish Coffee?? Lavendar Matcha?? Could be anything!
Location/Parking
Here we rate how accessible the location is to get to and park at! Have you ever seen a coffee shop that you thought looked great on TikTok but when you get there you find out you have to park 15 minutes away! No more!
Music/Vibes
Who wants to sit in a coffee shop in dead silence?? This ain't the 8th floor of Geisel! We want good music and even better vibes because everybody knows the better the vibes, the better the studying.
Value
Sure any coffee shop can be amazing, but can they all be amazing without breaking the bank?? We're college students and while a $8 $11 latte once in a while won't hurt, we're not made of money people.
Outlets/Convenience
This may our single biggest pet peeve of coffee shops, not enough outlets! Look not all of us have brand-new Mac-book airs that can go 3 weeks without a charge, some of us have power hungry devices, and we need those outlets!
Food
During those long study sessions sometimes a liquid diet won't just cut it! How good is the food??? Is that $18 avocado toast actually worth it?? A bagel is really only $2?? We aim to answer those questions!
Accessibility
And lastly, accessibility! Everyone deserves to be able to visit any coffee shop they want and we want people to know if they can!
